Our goal is to eliminate avoidable food
waste in commercial kitchens through
the use of state of the art analytics.

←The Problem

The hospitality and food service sector throws out over 365 million tonnes of food annually. This mountain of waste has both a huge environmental impact and is a financial detriment to buisnesses. However over 90% of this waste is completely avoidable.

→Flowaste

By using computer vision we can analyse, classify and quantify the waste being produced in these establishments then feed this information back to the management. With this wealth of information they can then make informed decisions about their menu, portion sizes and ordering.

←Impact
